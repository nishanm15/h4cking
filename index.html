    Kali External Wifi Card Guide
    Requires specific hardware
  • Intermediate

    `airodump-ng` (scan networks, capture packets, target specific BSSID/channel), `aireplay-ng` (deauthentication attacks to force handshake), `aircrack-ng` (crack WEP keys or WPA/WPA2 handshakes).

    Aircrack-ng Documentation
    THM: Wifi Hacking 101
  • Advanced

    Use `airodump-ng` to target an AP and capture the 4-way handshake (occurs when client connects/reconnects). Use `aireplay-ng` deauth attack to speed up capture. Use `aircrack-ng` or `hashcat` with a wordlist to crack the PSK offline.

  • Intermediate

    Reaver (Fork) / Bully
    THM: Wifi Hacking 101 (Covers WPS)
